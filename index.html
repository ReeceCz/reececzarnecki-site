About Me

I'm a builder at the intersection of mathematics, trading, and high-stakes finance. While completing a triple major at the University of Jamestown, I've founded Czarnecki Capital, engineered low-latency trading systems, and scaled a landscaping company into a profitable business. My work centers on quantitative strategy, algorithmic execution, and disciplined capital deployment guided by precision, scale, and a results-first mindset.

Featured Projects

MarketLabs — Market Terminal, Analytics, & Test Bench

MarketLabs unifies market data, portfolio insights, and risk frameworks into a single fast interface. Designed as a test bench, it accelerates the cycle from idea → test → decision, with built-in paths to deploy strategies and tools into live environments. By closing the gap between research and execution, it ensures insights don’t stay stuck in the lab.

Advanced Portfolio Analytics Engine

Predictive modeling for portfolio risk, return, and scenario outcomes, with tools for factor attribution, stress testing, and forward-looking simulations.

EquityLens AI

An AI-powered equity research engine that combines neural networks, NLP, and financial modeling to detect market inefficiencies, auto-parse SEC filings, and generate institutional-grade valuation theses with price targets.
